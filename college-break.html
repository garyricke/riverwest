FAQ

Things students ask.

What counts as "non-local"?

If your school is far enough away that you can't reasonably commute home to work out — that's non-local. We're not strict about distance; we're looking for students who are genuinely living away at school and home for breaks.

What if I have an online or local program?

The Break Pass is built for students who go away. If you live nearby and attend school locally (online or in-person), check out our standard memberships — there's likely a better fit.

When does the year start?

Your year starts the day you sign up and runs 12 months. Buy it in December? It runs through next December. Buy it before summer? Covers next year's winter and spring breaks too.

Can I bring a friend or family member?

Guest passes work the same as any membership. Your family can join under our standard plans — and yes, our annual plan comes with the "guaranteed or your old body back" promise.

What if I drop out, transfer, or graduate mid-year?

Your pass stays valid through the 12 months you paid for. After that, if you're back in Batavia full-time, we'll roll you onto a standard membership at the resident rate.
